Our vision is a future where a large share of the adult market uses one unified token; Bunny, for all their transactions. The different sectors of the industry will create an intricate ecosystem with thousands of interconnected businesses, in which the token will be exchanged, and its value will be consistently on the rise. Our aim is for BunnyToken to become the principal cryptocurrency which will largely replace fiat currencies in the multibillion dollar adult industry over the course of several years. BunnyToken will serve as a solution to a prominent problem, becoming a highly used and valuable token for millions of businesses and individuals.
